You are geeting closer to a chance for a better life...
Now you need to decide from which city will you be attempting to cross the border.

The two possible cities are:

1. Mexicali
    - Price to attempt to cross is lower    
    - More dangerous
    - Shorter path to cross
    - Longer trip to L.A.

2. Tijuana 
    - Price to attempt to cross is higher 
    - Higher chance of getting caught
    - Longer path to cross
    - Shorter trip to L.A.
